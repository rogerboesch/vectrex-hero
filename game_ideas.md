# Game Ideas

Based on the room-based engine with tile/wall rendering, enemies, player physics, collectibles, and hazards.

## 1. Cave Diver
Underwater cave exploration. Oxygen depletes as a timer. Air pockets in certain rooms refill it. Currents push the player in specific directions. Collect pearls for score.

## 2. Lava Escape
Volcano is erupting. Player starts at the BOTTOM and must climb UP through rooms. Lava rises steadily from below — if it catches you, game over. Choose between fast risky paths or slower safe ones.

## 3. Pharaoh's Tomb
Egyptian pyramid with keys and locked doors. Each room may have a colored key or matching gate. Mummies patrol corridors. Find the sarcophagus treasure at the deepest room.

## 4. Robot Factory
Navigate a malfunctioning robot through a factory. Conveyor belts move the player automatically. Crushers act as timed hazards. Reach the repair station before your battery dies.

## 5. Ant Colony
Play as an ant carrying food back to the queen. Rooms are tunnels with branching paths. Spider enemies try to steal your food. Pheromone trail shows rooms you've visited.

## 6. Dragon's Hoard
A thief descending into a dragon's lair. Collect gold in every room. The dragon patrols the bottom rooms. Sneak past or use smoke bombs (limited supply) to blind it.

## 7. Ice Caves
Slippery physics on all surfaces. Player slides until hitting a wall. Icicle hazards fall from ceilings. Rescue frozen explorers trapped in ice blocks (use dynamite like H.E.R.O.'s bombs).

## 8. Sewer Rat
Play as a rat navigating city sewers. Water rises and falls periodically in rooms. Cats patrol as enemies. Collect cheese scattered through rooms. Reach the surface.

## 9. Space Station Breach
Hull breach on a space station. Rooms depressurize one by one from the bottom up. Seal breaches by reaching control panels. Floating debris acts as hazards in zero-G rooms.

## 10. Mine Cart Madness
Horizontal rooms become mine cart rides with auto-scrolling. Player ducks/jumps obstacles. Vertical shafts are traditional platforming. Collect gems in carts, rescue trapped miners at dead ends.

## 11. Haunted Mansion
Each room is dark until the player enters. Ghosts are invisible until you shine your lantern (limited fuel). Find candles to light rooms permanently. Rescue the family trapped in the basement.

## 12. Jungle Temple
Indiana Jones-style. Rooms have dart traps, rolling boulders, and collapsing floors. Collect idol artifacts. A rival explorer races you through a parallel path — beat them to the treasure.

## 13. Wizard's Tower
Climb a wizard's tower collecting spell scrolls. Each scroll gives a temporary power: freeze enemies, walk through walls, double jump. The wizard at the top is the final boss.

## 14. Submarine Descent
Pilot a mini-sub into an ocean trench. Pressure increases per room (hull integrity timer). Anglerfish and jellyfish enemies. Sonar pings reveal hidden passages. Rescue deep-sea researchers.

## 15. Prison Break
Escape a multi-level prison. Guards patrol with flashlight cones. Timing-based stealth through rooms. Find tools (crowbar, wire cutters) to open different barrier types. Alarm triggers if spotted.

## 16. Treasure Hunter
Competitive scoring. Rooms have randomized treasure values. Limited dynamite to break walls. The twist: a rival AI hunter enters from the opposite side and races for the same loot.

## 17. Mushroom Caves
Bounce on mushroom platforms to reach higher exits. Spore clouds obscure vision periodically. Collect glowing fungi to light dark rooms. Poison mushrooms damage you — learn which colors are safe.

## 18. Clock Tower
Vertical tower with gear mechanisms. Rotating gears act as platforms and crushers. Bells ring periodically stunning all enemies. Repair the broken clock at the top by finding 4 gear pieces across rooms.

## 19. Alien Hive
Colonial marines enter an alien hive to rescue survivors. Eggs hatch if you get too close. Acid drips replace lava. Motion tracker shows enemy count per adjacent room. Ammo is limited — use it wisely.

## 20. Dwarf Mine
Dig your own path downward by choosing which walls to destroy. Each wall type hides different things: ore (score), gems (bonus), gas pockets (hazard), or water (floods the room temporarily). Strategic digging vs speed.
